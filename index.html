Spotify Canvas — full guide

What is a Spotify Canvas?

A Spotify Canvas is a 3–8 second vertical looping video that replaces a track's cover art in the Now Playing view on the Spotify mobile app. Spotify launched Canvas in 2019 and made it free for every artist. Tracks with a Canvas see a measurable lift in shares, saves, playlist adds and time spent. Canvas only plays on iOS and Android — not on desktop, web player, or smart speakers.

Canvas is uploaded through Spotify for Artists, not through your distributor. You don't need DistroKid, Tunecore, Amuse or CD Baby to add it — only access to the Spotify for Artists team for the track.

How to make a Spotify Canvas

  1. Pick the Canvas format tab at the top.
  2. Drop a photo (JPG/PNG/WEBP) or video (MP4/MOV/WEBM). For a still photo, pick an effect — Slow zoom, Pan, Breathe, or Static.
  3. Adjust crop. For video, set Start/End — clip must be 3–8 seconds.
  4. Tick Seamless loop and pick crossfade length (0.3s / 0.5s / 0.8s).
  5. Click Export MP4. Encodes locally via WebCodecs H.264. Upload to Spotify for Artists → track → Canvas.

How to upload to Spotify

  1. Sign in at artists.spotify.com.
  2. Open Music → Releases, pick the track.
  3. Click the Canvas tab.
  4. Upload the 9:16 MP4. Save and publish.

Loop styles

Spotify's guidelines describe three: Continuous Loop — end blends into start (what this tool produces). Hard Cut — visible restart jitter. Rebound — ping-pong forward/reverse. Continuous looks the most professional.

Header / Avatar / Cover Art

Header sits at the top of your artist page. Min 2660×1140. Artist name + avatar overlay the lower-left — keep that area visually clean.

Avatar is the round profile picture. Min 750×750, rec 1500×1500. Center your subject.

FAQ — 16 common questions
What are the exact Spotify Canvas dimensions?

Vertical 9:16 MP4. Min 720×1280, rec 1080×1920. Duration 3–8 seconds.

How long does a Spotify Canvas have to be?

3 to 8 seconds. Outside that range is rejected at upload.

What is the aspect ratio for Spotify Canvas?

9:16 — same vertical ratio as TikTok / Reels. The crop frame is locked to 9:16.

Is spotifyedits.com really free, with no watermark?

Yes. No signup, no email, no watermark. Runs entirely in your browser via WebCodecs.

How do I make a seamless looping Canvas?

Tick "Seamless loop" before export and pick a crossfade length. The tool blends last frames into first frames using cosine easing.

What Spotify formats does this tool support?

Canvas (9:16 MP4), Header (2660×1140), Avatar (750×750 or 1500×1500), Cover Art (1600×1600 or 3000×3000).

Why does my Canvas upload keep failing?

Three common reasons: wrong aspect ratio (must be 9:16), wrong duration (3–8 sec), or wrong format (must be MP4). This tool enforces all three.

What is the Spotify profile picture / avatar size?

Min 750×750, rec 1500×1500 for retina. JPEG or PNG, sRGB, square.

What is the Spotify Header size?

2660×1140 min (≈2.33:1), JPEG or PNG. Artist name + avatar overlay the lower-left.

What size should Spotify Cover Art be?

1:1 square, 3000×3000 ideal. JPEG, sRGB, under 10 MB.
