SEO guide + verified specs

Spotify Canvas, Header, Avatar and Cover Art specs in one place.

PrivacyThe preview can run locally in the browser; no upload is needed for the mock flow.

Canvas

Spotify Canvas is a 3–8 second vertical MP4 in the mobile Now Playing view. Recommended export is 1080×1920, 9:16, muted.

Header

Spotify artist Header should be at least 2660×1140. Wide profile banner — avoid important text in the lower-left overlay area.

Avatar

Spotify profile pictures are square images shown as a circle. 750×750 minimum, but center the subject for safe crop.

Cover Art

Spotify Cover Art is square. Distributors prefer 3000×3000 sRGB JPEG, under 10 MB, with no important content cut at edges.

Common questions

Answers artists look for before making Spotify visuals.

What changes when I choose another format?

The specs, upload copy, preview frame, export checklist and CTA all change inside the same workspace. That makes it clear what you are preparing.

Can I start from a Pexels video?

Yes. Pexels is useful when you do not have your own footage yet. Pick a mood, download a free vertical or cinematic clip, then crop it into Canvas or another Spotify visual format.

Where does Spotify Canvas appear?

Canvas appears in Spotify's mobile Now Playing view on iOS and Android. It does not replace the desktop cover art.

Do I need a distributor to upload Canvas?

No. Canvas is managed in Spotify for Artists. You still need access to the artist profile or team for the track.

Is my video uploaded to a server?

No. All processing happens locally in your browser via WebCodecs H.264. Nothing is sent anywhere — verifiable by going offline after the page loads.

How long should a Spotify Canvas be?

3 to 8 seconds. Anything outside that range is rejected at upload. The trim slider in this tool prevents you from going over.
